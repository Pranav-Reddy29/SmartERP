# SmartERP

## Project Overview

SmartERP is a cloud-based Billing, Inventory, and Accounting Management System inspired by Tally.

The application allows businesses to manage:

- Accounting
- Inventory
- Billing
- Purchases
- Sales
- GST
- Customer Management
- Supplier Management
- Reports

## MVP Features

### Authentication
- Register
- Login
- JWT Authentication

### Company Management
- Create Company
- Update Company
- Delete Company
- Select Company

### Masters
- Groups
- Ledgers
- Units
- Stock Groups
- Stock Items

### Transactions
- Purchase Voucher
- Sales Voucher

### Billing
- GST Invoice
- Quotation
- Estimate
- Proforma Invoice

### Reports
- Sales Reports
- Purchase Reports
- Stock Summary

### Keyboard Navigation
- F8 Sales
- F9 Purchase
- ALT + L Ledger
- CTRL + K Search
